Healthier Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a healthy alternative to mac and cheese that you can make at home. It has bread crumbs and whole wheat noodles to make it extra delicious.

This is the perfect ooey-gooey meal for anyone who is trying to cut back on carbs and dairy. Serve with a salad for a complete dinner.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces of whole wheat macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 2.5 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups low-fat milk
  • 0.5 cup whole wheat bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Boil a large pot of slightly salted water. Cook macorini in water, stirring occasionally until cooked through. Drain.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in saucepan over medium heat. Stir flour in to make a roux. Add milk slowly. Stir in cheddar and parmesan cheeses. Cook until cheese is melted and sauce is thick. Place macaroni in large baking dish and pour sauce over macaroni.
  3. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and stir until butter is absorbed. Spread over macaroni and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in oven until cheese sauce is hot and breadcrumbs are browned. Roughly 30 minutes.
